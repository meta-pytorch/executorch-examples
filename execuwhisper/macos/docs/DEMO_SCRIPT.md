# ExecuWhisper Live Demo — Script

---

## Scene 1 — Intro (on camera)

> Hi, this is ExecuWhisper — an open-source Mac dictation app built on Meta's ExecuTorch.

---

## Scene 2 — The pitch (on camera)

> Think of it as a free, fully on-device version of Wispr Flow and SuperWhisper. No subscription, no cloud, nothing leaves your laptop.

---

## Scene 3 — The differentiator (on camera, lead into screen)

> And like SuperWhisper, it has smart formatting — not just speech to text, but actually clean, finished writing. Let me show you.

---

## Scene 4 — Demo 1: shopping list (screen)

*(Press hotkey, speak naturally.)*

**Spoken:**

> here is the list that I need to buy banana apple grape and orange

**Auto-pasted result:**

> Here is the list that I need to buy: banana, apple, grape, and orange.

**Voiceover:**

> Notice the colon and the commas? I never said any of those out loud — the model figured out it was a list.

---

## Scene 5 — Demo 2: disfluencies and duplicates (screen)

*(Press hotkey. Speak with natural fillers — let the *uhs* be real.)*

**Spoken:**

> uh can we, can we move the meeting uh to Friday, actually no, let's let's make it Monday at 10 am

**Auto-pasted result:**

> Can we move the meeting to Friday? Actually, no — let's make it Monday at 10 AM.

**Voiceover:**

> All the *uhs* are gone. The "can we, can we" and "let's let's" duplicates collapsed into one. Question mark and time format added automatically.

---

## Scene 6 — Outro (on camera)

> The DMG is in the description. Download it, drop it in your Applications folder, and it works on your Mac right now.
